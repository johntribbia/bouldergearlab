Overall: Value for the Money

The TENWAYS CGO600 Pro is a good value for the money. It’s a lightweight, affordable, and stylish eBike that’s perfect for city commuting or leisurely rides. The CGO600 Pro also has a powerful motor, long battery life, and comfortable ride. At $1,999, the CGO600 Pro is a reasonable price for lightweight eBikes. The greatest appeal of the bike is its simple design and the incredibly low maintenance it requires as well as the integrated lighting. This is a bike that can withstand the weather with long lasting and easy to maintain parts such as the Gates carbon belt drive, disk brakes, fenders, and automatic lights.

The CGO600 Pro is a bit unidimensional with a rigid front fork, but it makes up for the bumpy riding in how lightweight it is overall - it is an excellent option for roads and bike paths. With only a single speed gearing, the bike is powerful enough to keep pace with stop and go traffic as well as ascend steeper inclines on full power. As a frequent commuter, I love the fenders for wet riding conditions. With the 360kW battery, the range on the bike is excellent compared to other similarly classed eBikes.
