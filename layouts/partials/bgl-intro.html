Real Testing, Real Insights

Every review at Boulder Gear Lab combines analytical thinking with extensive real-world testing. As a data scientist who's also a lifelong runner and cyclist, I bring a critical eye to gear evaluation - not through lab measurements, but through thousands of miles on Colorado's trails, roads, and mountains.

Good gear reviews require extensive field testing, comparative thinking, and honest assessment of both strengths and weaknesses. My background as a data scientist shapes how I think about products - asking the right questions, considering edge cases, and thinking critically about performance claims.

Every review is independent, with no brand sponsorships influencing recommendations. The goal is to share real-world experience that helps you make informed decisions.
